Who am I?

I'm Marius and do photography.
I've been in love with photography since I took my first picture and from then on I knew what makes me happy. I do this as a hobby and I get to enjoy every second of it along the people who follow my activity on social media.

Recently I got into photographing the night sky beside the basic day time photography and I'm slowly making the transition towards the night sky. If you have ever witnessed an incredible image of space taken using an ordinary camera or just found yourself staring at the night sky and thought “I want to do that”, you’re not alone.

How I started astrophotography?

In a few words, photography in general is my hobby that someday I would like to become my job. I enjoy every bit of it and do it with passion. My passion for astrophotography started a long time ago, watching other people's videos on YouTube but only got to it 1 year ago, in December 2019. It all started with the basic equipment and the desire to capture amazing things like a already saw in those cool videos I watched and share them with my closest people.

Astronomy in general fascinated me since a young kid. I always stared at the night sky and especially the moon being the easiest thing to watch on most of the nights with your eyes. My knowledge and understanding of astronomy and photography are now combining to help me achieve even more in the field of astrophotography. Mainly because of me growing up and dedicating more time understanding the phenomenons, I already started to identify multiple constelations that I already saw but not knowing their names.
